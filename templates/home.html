Core Platform Features

  • Synonym-Aware Skill Matching:

    Recognizes that 'AWS' is 'Amazon Web Services' and matches variations intelligently instead of doing pure substring matches.

  • Honeypot Validation Layer:

    Guards the pipeline from fake/impossible candidate profiles (expert in 10 skills with 0 months experience) by flagging anomalies immediately.

  • Two-Stage Retrieve & Rank:

    Implements a lightning-fast pre-screening filter to drop the candidate pool from 100,000 to the top 2,000 before running full ML encoding, keeping execution times under 3 minutes.

  • Explainable AI scoring:

    Fills out structural reasons, strength highlights, and gaps. Generates the 1-2 sentence reasoning required for recruitment shortlists.
